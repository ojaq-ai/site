Doctrine

Agents, not assistants.

Ojaq is not building prompt wrappers, copilot features, or generic assistants. It is building a family of agents with clear identity, legible specifications, and a product path that extends beyond software.

The company starts in software because software is the fastest path to a working agent. But the company is not ultimately defined by software. It is defined by the quality of the agents and the forms they inhabit.

Form Class

Whether the agent begins in software, a still product, or a moving product and how it evolves.

Family

Different Ojaq agents can be tuned for different roles and social styles while still belonging to one coherent product system.

Conversational

Guide

Composed, articulate, and socially fluent. Designed for explanation, translation, and elegant presence.

Operational

Continuity

Companion

Persistent, context-rich, and memory-first. Designed to carry identity and continuity across time.

Forms

Two form families.

Ojaq starts with a simple split. Still forms bring calm presence into a place. Moving forms carry the same agent identity through space and time.

Still

Products that hold presence.

Still forms should feel quiet, precise, and native to the environments they live in.

  • Room PresenceShared spaces where the agent becomes a calm layer of coordination.
  • Desk PresenceFocused environments where the agent remains available without taking over the workspace.
  • Ambient PresenceSubtle interaction where the product fades into the background until needed.
Moving

Products that carry identity through motion.

Moving forms should retain the same clarity and product discipline while operating in the world.

  • Personal CompanionMovement across the day with continuity, context, and coordination.
  • Field MobilityOperational settings where the agent moves with the work instead of waiting at a desk.
  • Physical AutonomyFuture products that navigate space while staying minimal, legible, and useful.
