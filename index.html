Doctrine

A serious agent product should be legible.

Most AI products collapse into one generic assistant. Ojaq is built around the opposite idea: distinct agents, clear specifications, and a disciplined path from software to still and moving forms.

Software is only the first proving ground. It is where the agent can be refined fastest, where the loop can be tested live, and where product language can become precise.

The company is not ultimately defined by a dashboard or a workflow feature. It is defined by the quality of the agents and the forms they inhabit.

Agents first. Forms next.

That is the simplest version of the company doctrine and the standard the rest of the product system has to meet.
Specification system

The agent comes with a profile.

Ojaq agents should be understood the way great products are understood: through a clear profile, not abstract capability claims.

A future lineup should feel spec-driven, not persona-driven. The important question is not whether an agent sounds different. The important question is how it thinks, acts, remembers, and where it lives first.

01Memory

How much continuity the agent carries and how well it preserves context over time.

02Intelligence

The quality of reasoning, judgment, and prioritization under live conditions.

03Initiative

Its social stance: whether it waits, guides, intervenes, or acts with confidence.

04Voice

Its spoken presence, cadence, and clarity when the product operates through audio.

05Tools

What the agent can access, operate, and orchestrate across systems and environments.

06Form class

Whether the product begins in software, a still form, or a moving form and how it evolves.

Form path

One core. Three product surfaces.

The family begins in software, extends into still products that hold presence, and then into moving products that carry the same identity through the world.

Software The first working surface.

The fastest path to refining the agent loop, product language, and specification system.

Still Products that hold presence.

Calm, precise forms that remain native to their environment instead of turning the room into a gadget.

Moving Products that carry identity.

Forms that preserve the same agent character through motion, continuity, and real-world action.

Refine the core before the form.

Software comes first because it is the fastest way to make the agent better. It lets Ojaq harden memory, judgment, initiative, and voice before the family expands into still and moving products.

This keeps the company grounded in product quality rather than concept art. The form should inherit a strong agent, not compensate for a weak one.
